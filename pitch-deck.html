Communication Stack Evolution

Phase 0-3 (0-500K users): Socket.io, Twilio, Agora, Resend

Phase 4 (500K-1M users): Migrate to managed services

  • Socket.io → Ably (saves $17.8k/mo)

  • Video: Agora P2P + enterprise (70% savings)
  • Email: Resend → Amazon SES (50% savings)
