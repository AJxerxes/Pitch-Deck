Investor FAQ

Q: Do you validate or assign a user’s gender?

No. Verification is authenticity-only (liveness). Users select their preferred community (Men’s or Women’s) and can change it later. DuoWeave never assigns or validates gender.

Q: Why mandatory verification instead of optional like Bumble?

Optional verification doesn't solve the trust problem—it creates two-tier systems where verified users are rare. Mandatory verification changes the platform psychology: everyone knows everyone is real. That's the moat.

Q: What about privacy concerns with facial recognition?

We verify liveness and identity—no long-term biometric storage. Privacy-by-default with GDPR/CCPA compliance. Verification data is hashed and encrypted; users control profile data sharing.

Q: How do you prevent fake verification (photos of photos)?

Liveness detection requires real-time movement and multiple angles with challenge-response (blink, head turn, smile). Current testing accuracy: 99.2%. Improving with InsightFace/FaceNet.

Q: Won't gender-specific spaces face legal/PR issues?

We frame these as community preference options—users choose their experience. It supports comfort and safety, especially for women. Anyone can join either community; a 90-day cooldown applies between preference changes. Inclusive options for non-binary users are part of the roadmap.

Q: What if Meta/Instagram adds mandatory verification?

Unlikely: it would remove inflated metrics from bot/fake accounts that power ad revenue. Once DuoWeave is known as the verified platform, network effects keep users despite copycat features.

Q: What's your user acquisition cost?

Organic (campus ambassadors): ~\$5 per verified user. Paid ads: ~\$15 per verified user. With 5% premium conversion, LTV is \$120. That's 24:1 LTV:CAC organic (8:1 paid) — industry-leading.

Q: Why students first instead of broader market?

Students are dense and accessible, have high connection needs, are early adopters, and drive strong word-of-mouth. After campuses, we expand to young professionals in the same cities.

Q: What if initial campus launch fails?

Fallbacks: launch at another university, target specific communities (gamers/fitness/hobbies), pivot to B2B verified spaces, or focus on events-first. Our verification tech is valuable across use cases.

Q: How do you compete with free platforms?

We compete on trust, not price. Free tier builds the network; premium monetizes power users. Verified audiences earn 5–8x higher CPMs, reducing pressure to aggressively monetize users.

Q: What's your long-term vision?

Become the identity verification layer for the internet. Start with social, expand to events, then offer a verification API: "Login with DuoWeave" = guaranteed real human. $10B+ potential.

Q: Can users change their community selection?

Yes. Community selection is user-controlled and changeable in Settings. To promote stability and reduce abuse, changes are limited to once every 90 days.

Q: Why raise only $65K?

Capital efficiency. We can prove the model (500–5000 users, PMF signals, unit economics) with $65K. This derisks the next round and supports a higher seed valuation or YC acceptance.
